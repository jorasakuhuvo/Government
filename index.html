Thai Helicopters Circling Near Jorasakuhuvo's Airspace Today

Starting from 14:48 to 14:51 on V.T. 2090 Natuno 3 Syva 14, Jorasakuhuvo observed Thai helicopters circling three times in succession near Jorasakuhuvo's airspace from the ground.

The Condemnation Includes Any Act of Publicly Humiliating Vasians

Jorasakuhuvo emphasized that prohibiting the humiliation of citizens does not mean that people cannot offer reasonable advice, suggestions, or criticism. Rather, it means that expressions should avoid tones or language that may cause psychological or emotional harm to others.

The President Has Been Repeatedly Attacked with Rubber by Foreigners

On the afternoon of V.T. 2090 Natuno 3 Syva 9, Pemife Noraane was struck in the back with a rubber bullet at least three times. An investigation into suspected perpetrators is ongoing. Jorasakuhuvo stated that this was a less-than-honorable act of opposition to the president.
